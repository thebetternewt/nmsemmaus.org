A big “THANK YOU” to Larry Woodward and the Class of 2004 for a job well done in service on the North Mississippi Emmaus Community Board of Directors. Last year was a tremendous year for North Mississippi Emmaus. Under the leadership of the Holy Spirit we celebrated 20 miraculous years of Emmaus and then God opened the door for us to extend our boundaries to the country of Honduras.
I appreciate the many hours of service and dedication it took in both of these huge projects. “Nothing is impossible with God” and He continues to prove it year after year to all who believe and trust Him.
Our partnership with Costa Rica to help start a new Emmaus Community in Honduras has begun. In November of 2004, we had the privilege to sponsor 10 Honduran men and women to attend a Walk to Emmaus in Costa Rica. Like all Walks, blessings were abundant for the pilgrims. This year we have again committed to support this effort. Our agape for these pilgrims is to provide their expenses, which include airfare, lodging and food. Our contribution for these walks is $5.000.
We need your financial support, your prayers and agape to complete this blessing God has made available to us as an Emmaus Community.
Donations to the Honduras Emmaus Project may be mailed to:
Harry Lee Robinson, P.O. Box 228, Winona, MS 38967

In His Service,
