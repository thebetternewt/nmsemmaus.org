IMMEDIATE NEED AT CHRISTIAN CAMP OF NORTH MISSISSIPPI

 

As most know, our Emmaus and Chrysalis Walks are held at Christian Camp of

North Mississippi. This is a separate organization that works behind the

scenes (much like the committees do on a weekend walk) to make certain that

our Emmaus/Chrysalis teams and, most importantly, the pilgrims and

caterpillars on these Walks have the very best accommodations possible on

their three-day walks. These facilities have been available for our Walks

since 1982 and have been maintained through the generous support of our

Emmaus and Chrysalis families who recognize and support this endeavor.

Presently, the six park benches that pilgrims enjoy as they awaken each

morning and/or await being led back to the conference room are unsafe and

must be replaced. It is not feasible or cost efficient to try and repair

them. New, metal benches are available at a cost of $100.00 each. The CCNM

Board would like to make communities and/or individuals aware of this need

and, if you are led to support this and/or other financial needs at the

camp, please feel free to do so by sending your gift to CCNM, 414 Blaine

Road, Sunflower, MS 38778.

Thank you for your continued support of CCNM.

DeColores.
